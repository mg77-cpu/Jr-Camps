## 2. Who We Serve (Our Partners)
We operate as a B2B Solution for organizations that need youth programming but don't have the staff or expertise to run it themselves.
* **For Schools**: We provide a turnkey after-school solution. We handle the insurance, hiring, and registration, allowing the school to offer enrichment without adding work to their front office.
* **For Cities & Rec Centers**: We fill their "Activity Guides" with reliable programming, operating on a revenue-share model that generates income for the municipality.
* **For Communities (HOAs/Apartments)**: We provide on-site resident services that increase tenant retention and meet government compliance (LURA) requirements for affordable housing.

## 3. The "Turnkey" Promise
The biggest differentiator of Jr Camps is that we handle everything.
* **We bring the Staff**: All coaches are Live Scan background-checked, trained, and CPR certified.
* **We bring the Gear**: From soccer balls to robotics kits, we bring the equipment to the site.
* **We handle the Admin**: Our digital platform handles liability waivers, payments, and parent communication.
